NEWS RELEASE: MRDA INFO TEAM Championship
Read More

CALABANGA, CAMARINES SUR — The MRDA Academic Center, Inc. INFO TEAM has once again proven their excellence by securing the Championship title in the Infomercial Contest during the Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP) Community Relation Week. The event took place on March 6, 2026, at the BFP Calabanga Compound in San Isidro.

This victory marks another milestone for the INFO TEAM, who last year brought pride to both the town of Calabanga and the Province of Camarines Sur by representing the province in the Regional Contest and earning a 2nd Place finish. Their consistent performance solidifies MRDA's reputation for media and communication prowess.

The institution also celebrated success in other cultural and literary events: Ms. Lyka Mendoza (Grade 11): 3rd Place – Spoken Word Poetry Poster Making Contest: 4th Place Jingle Making Contest: 4th Place.

The MRDA administration expressed deep gratitude to the dedicated coaches who guided the students to success:Mr. Billy Jose Z. Perit III, Mr. Jay Mark Belaje.

The institution extends its warmest congratulations to all the winners and participants. The INFO TEAM is now looking forward to representing the town once again in the next level of competition.
