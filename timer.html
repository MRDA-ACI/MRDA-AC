What is the Study Heatmap? ▼

About the Heatmap

The Study Heatmap shows how much you studied each day. Each square represents one day of study activity from your completed timer sessions.

Color Guide

  • ⬜ Gray – No study sessions
  • 🟩 Light Green – Short study time
  • 🟩🟩 Medium Green – Moderate study time
  • 🟩🟩🟩 Dark Green – High study time

Try to keep your heatmap filled by studying consistently every day to maintain your focus streak.
