Welcome to MRDA AcademeAccess

MRDA Academic Center Inc. was established in 2018 in Calabanga, Camarines Sur. With a commitment to excellence, the institution has grown into a beacon of education, empowering students from diverse backgrounds to reach their full potential. Our mission is to foster holistic development, ensuring each learner excels academically, socially, and personally.
